Stasis

The knights' cold shadows looming,
A desperate fight, evermore in stasis.
I pick a moonlit flower blooming,
Unthinking of the long past crisis.

This short poem was inspired by a painting I once saw. The poem depicts what I saw in the painting, which was of a dark scene where a young girl picked flowers, seemingly unaware of the stone battle surrounding her.

Personal Interests

I love music. I've played piano for most of my life, and have recently been learning to play the organ. Some of my favorite piano pieces I have learned / am learning include Liebestraume no. 3 by Franz Liszt, Sweet Remembrance of You by William Joseph, and An American in Paris by George Gershwin.

I love books. I love reading -- especially science fiction and fantasy -- and I enjoy researching new things. I also love writing. I love creating worlds and exploring themes applicable to our world through writing.

Recently, I have begun teaching myself digital painting. I've always loved art (and, honestly, anything creative, including cooking), and learned how to sketch with pencil and pen while I was fairly young. I love the depth and vibrancy of paintings, I so I've begun learning how to paint on my tablet.
